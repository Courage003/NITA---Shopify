About Us

This is a page dedicated to the whole student community of NIT Agartala, promoting the principles of reuse and recycle within the campus. Our goal is to help new batches obtain essential items at prices lower than the market value. The website is primarily focused on the regions around the campus.

Established in 2024, NITA Shopify aims to provide a platform where students can shop and find necessary items easily.

Mission

Our mission is to leverage the right technology to delight our customers. We aim to empower the lower-middle-class section to purchase important items at very reasonable prices. Additionally, we help the older batches recycle their items, such as graphics boards, stationery, notes, important PYQs, bedsheets, brooms, etc., and obtain some value for them.
